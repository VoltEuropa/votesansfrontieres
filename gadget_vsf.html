1. Can I vote?

EU citizens in France can only participate in Municipal elections (next one in 2025) and European elections (next one in 2024). Requirements:

  • at least 18 years old
  • EU citizen
  • resident in France
  • full civil and political rights.

Um zu wählen, muss man im jeweiligen Wahlregister seiner Kommune eingeschrieben sein (es gibt ein Register für jede Wahl).

2. What documents do I need?

The fastest way is online on https://www.demarches.interieur.gouv.fr/particuliers/elections-droit-vote-citoyen-europeen-france with the following documents:

  1. A copy of your passport, identity card or residency permit
  2. Justicatif de Domile - a document verifiying your address such as a recent electricity bill on your name (other possibilities).
  3. A declaration that you have full voting rights in your home country (Online generator)

or at your local Mairie (in person or by mail). Then you also need to complete the following forms:

  1. European Elections: Cerfa 12671-02
  2. Municipal Elections: Cerfa 12670-02

German Federal Elections, September 26th 2021

In order to vote, you need to sign up on the electoral list of the last municiality you lived in in Germany. Requirements:

  1. German nationality and at least 18 years old
  2. Lived at least 3 months in Germany during the last 25 years
  3. Or (!) because of a personal and direct familiarity with and being affected by political conditions (Examples).

The deadline (for receiving requests) for application is September 5th, 2021 (Timeline).
